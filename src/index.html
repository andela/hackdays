About

We believe that every engineer possesses amazing ideas and creativity. The #hackdays is a time for our engineers to stay up all night to create a working product or prototype from scratch. Remember - done is better than perfect!

Hackdays are open to all employees at Andela irrespective of where they may be in the world. In order to facilitate increased collaboration, we will have three venues in our campuses in the following countries.
